General Getting Started

What is the GEM T2 Controller Optimizer?

The GEM T2 Controller Optimizer is a web-based tool designed to help GEM electric vehicle owners optimize their T2 controller settings for improved performance, efficiency, and range. It uses AI-powered analysis to recommend optimal settings based on your vehicle configuration and driving needs.

Key Features:
  • PDF controller setting import
  • AI-powered optimization recommendations
  • Local profile management
  • Weekend trip planning
  • No account required - all data stays on your device

Is my data safe? Do you store my controller settings?

Absolutely! Your data privacy is our top priority. All your controller settings, profiles, and personal data are stored locally in your browser's storage - nothing is sent to our servers.
